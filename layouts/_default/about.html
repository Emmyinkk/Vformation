About V formation

Welcome to V Formation Consulting, the premier consulting firm dedicated to propelling your organization towards unparalleled success. Founded by Dr. Charles Drury, our mission is to elevate client organizations to operational supremacy by offering bespoke solutions that ignite passion and fulfill aspirations. We believe in the transformative power of unity, focusing on three pillars: spirit, health, and mind. Our vision is to elevate lives, ensuring everyone reaches their zenith potential through strategic wisdom and unwavering support.

At V Formation Consulting, we specialize in providing funding to help startups get off the ground, solve critical problems, and scale their businesses. We unite teams, filling in the missing pieces and guiding businesses toward excellence. Our expert advice and resources are designed to help you thrive in an ever-changing landscape. Fly with us towards extraordinary results, united as one, and experience the difference that true dedication and strategic insight can make.

What we do

  1. Funding PE and VC.
  2. Brand management (PR, Social Media, content Creation, digital marleting)
  3. Consumer and professional products
  4. Market Analysis
  5. Pitch decks and prep for raise
  6. Distribution and logistics
  7. Sales Enablement
  8. Lean Management
  9. Leadership Training and culture
